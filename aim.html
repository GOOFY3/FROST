Aims and Objective



The aims and objectives of the Association shall be:
 

  • To form a conglomeration of specialists, scientists, experts, technocrats in relation to river and water front
  • To provide a platform for generating a thought process for growth in respective fields
  • To generate technological proposals in this sector and submit it to the government for executive action
  • To enhance the awareness on the maritime environment & transportation
  • To generate awareness among administrators, bureaucrats, and public in general regarding rivers and oceans
  • To assist in enhancing the maritime safety of boats and small crafts plying in and around Odisha waters
  • To study the ecology of Odisha of both the coastal and inland waters
  • To build up a platform to discuss the safety regarding boats plying in Odisha waters & assist the Government agencies in providing the ideas to counter various hazards faced by the fishermen and boatmen of Odisha
  • To assist the Government in preparing a platform for maritime skill development
  • To work as Liaisoning Agency between the technocrats, the Government and other parties, who are concerned regarding the water resources and maritime sector
  • To represent the parties before the State and Union Government, Financial Institutions, Banks, Public and Private Organizations for the benefit of the State in general and country in whole
  • To accept Membership of any other society, association, persons, firms or anybody - corporate for promoting, or intending to promote in particular any of the objects of this association
  • To subscribe for, be a member of, and co-operate with any other association, institutions, firm, body corporate whose objects in whole, or in part, are similar to those of this Association
  • To give necessary guidance and co-operation for implementation of Projects and in other areas of operation of projects
  • To assist the Parties in obtaining various types of Technical and Financial Assistance available for the development of the maritime industry
  • To engage the services of Advocates, Accountants, Engineers, Consultants and such other experts on different areas on payment of fees, for the benefit of the Association

  • To acquire, purchase, take on lease or otherwise, land, building, other movable or immovable properties for the purpose of the Association
  • To accept gift, donation, subscription, funds on behalf of the Association and to invest the same in which the Association may think proper
  • To borrow money, take loan, receive advance for fulfilling the objects of the Association
  • To organize programs, seminars, symposiums on different subjects for the benefit of the Association and creating awareness
  • To do all necessary Acts, incidental and ancillary for the attainment of the objects of the Association.
