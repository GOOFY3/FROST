Activities



  • We have been meeting every month since 12-May-2016(our date of Inception) and discussing the several issues pertaining to the society and trying to work on it, as well as find solutions. Besides this, we are working at the ground level so that the issues can be identified, root cause analyzed and solutions though of after brain-storming sessions, workshops and seminars.
  • A seminar was conducted by us in association with the Seafarer's Association of Odisha on 18-jan-2017, on the Safety of Boats, where a list of recommendations have been drawn, which will be presented to the government, shortly. This attendees of the seminar included IIT Professors, Directors of renowned companies, Director of Water Transport, and several other state officers.
  • A workshop was conducted at Talcher on Ground Water Conservation, where people from all fraternities met and discussed about the drinking water problems associated with the area. The attendees included the Deputy Collector and other elected representatives of the area. A seminar on the same is being planned towards the end of the year.
  • We are conducting a National Semianr on "Natural Gas In Inland and Coastal Shipping", in collaboration with Institute of Engineers(India), Odisha State Centre on the 18th and 19th of November, 2017.
